Version 1: Full Feature PRD - Transport Excellence Management System (TEMS)
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose
This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the specifications for the Transport Excellence Management System (TEMS), a comprehensive ERP solution designed for transport and logistics companies operating in Africa. TEMS addresses the unique challenges of the African market while meeting global standards.

1.2 Product Vision
To create a world-class, AI-powered ERP system that transforms transport and logistics operations in Africa by providing end-to-end visibility, ensuring compliance with international standards, enabling data-driven decision making, and driving continuous improvement in safety, efficiency, and sustainability.

1.3 Scope
TEMS will be a modular ERP system covering all critical aspects of transport and logistics management:

Leadership & Governance
People & Competency
Fleet and Asset Management
Safety & Risk Management
Cross-Border Trade Management (Africa-specific)
Informal Economy Integration (Africa-specific)
Climate Resilience (Africa-specific)
2. Market Analysis
2.1 Target Users
Large transport corporations in Africa
Small and medium transport enterprises (SMEs)
Informal transport operators (motorcycle taxis, minibuses)
Transport cooperatives and associations
Government transport agencies
Non-profits working in transport and logistics
2.2 Market Needs
Resilient system that works offline and with low connectivity
Affordable solutions for SMEs and informal operators
Integration with mobile money and local payment systems
Adaptation to diverse regulatory environments across 54 African countries
Tools to manage cross-border trade complexities
Features to address climate and security risks
Support for multiple languages and cultural practices
3. Product Overview
3.1 Product Description
TEMS is a cloud-based, offline-first ERP solution specifically designed for the African transport and logistics market. It integrates all aspects of transport management into a single platform with resilience to infrastructure challenges and inclusivity for the informal economy.

3.2 Key Features

4. Functional Requirements
4.1 Module 1: Leadership & Governance
4.1.1 Vision & Strategy Management
Vision and Mission Statement Management: Create, store, and communicate vision/mission statements in multiple languages
Strategic Planning: Develop, document, and track short and long-term strategic goals with African market dynamics
KPI Management: Define, monitor, and report KPIs with African market benchmarks
Communication Tools: Ensure vision/strategy communication at all levels including voice notes for low-literacy users
4.1.2 Leadership Commitment
Meeting Management: Schedule, document, and track leadership meetings with offline capabilities
Spot-Check System: Plan, conduct, and document leadership spot-checks with photo evidence and GPS location
Incident Investigation Management: Assign leaders to investigation teams and track involvement
Accountability Tracking: Monitor leadership accountability with automated reminders
Continuous Improvement Initiatives: Propose, approve, and track improvement initiatives with impact analysis
4.1.3 Policies & Standards
Policy Management: Create, version control, and distribute policies with country-specific templates
Compliance Checking: Ensure policies align with local and international best practices with automated regulatory updates
Training Tracking: Document and monitor staff training with offline record keeping
Compliance Monitoring: Conduct regular checks against company policies with mobile inspection forms
4.1.4 Compliance Oversight
Audit Management: Schedule, conduct, and document compliance audits with offline capabilities
Regulatory Tracking: Monitor changes to transport regulations across African countries
Violation Reporting: Report and address non-compliance issues with anonymous reporting options
Compliance Metrics: Track, report, and use compliance data with predictive analytics
4.2 Module 2: People & Competency
4.2.1 Recruitment, Training & Qualification
Driver Qualification Management: Track licenses, records, medical fitness, and assessments with digital verification
Training Management: Develop, schedule, and track training programs with offline content access
Competency Matrix: Define and track required competencies with skills gap analysis
Training Effectiveness: Evaluate training effectiveness with mobile assessments
Certification Tracking: Monitor certifications and renewal dates with automated reminders
4.2.2 Performance Management
Job Description Management: Create and maintain job descriptions with role-specific templates
KPI Assignment: Define and assign KPIs with industry benchmarks
Performance Reviews: Conduct and document performance reviews with mobile capabilities
Recognition System: Recognize high performers with mobile money rewards
Performance Analytics: Use performance data for decisions with predictive insights
4.2.3 Safety Culture & Engagement
Meeting Management: Schedule and document toolbox meetings with voice recording for low-literacy users
Issue Resolution: Track and resolve safety meeting issues with escalation workflows
Safety Reporting: Report unsafe conditions with photo and video evidence
Feedback System: Structured safety suggestions with USSD access
Safety Culture Assessment: Assess and improve safety culture with anonymous surveys
4.2.4 Succession Planning
Critical Role Identification: Identify key roles with risk assessment
Talent Development: Create talent development plans with mobile learning paths
High-Potential Tracking: Monitor and mentor high-potential employees with coaching tools
Succession Review: Review and update succession plans with scenario planning
4.3 Module 3: Fleet and Asset Management
4.3.1 Asset Availability & Reliability
Asset Inventory: Maintain a comprehensive inventory of all fleet vehicles and assets with offline access and photo documentation
Asset Tracking: Monitor location and operational status in real-time with GPS and offline location caching
Availability Metrics: Track and report on asset availability and uptime with predictive analytics
Reliability Metrics: Monitor MTBF and other reliability indicators with failure trend analysis
Spare Parts Management: Manage spare parts inventory with barcode/QR code scanning and low-stock alerts
4.3.2 Maintenance Systems
Maintenance Scheduling: Plan and schedule preventive and predictive maintenance with automated reminders
Maintenance Tracking: Document all maintenance activities and history with offline capability
Work Order Management: Create, assign, and track maintenance work orders with mobile access
Predictive Maintenance: Use AI to predict maintenance needs based on operational data and vehicle sensor inputs
Maintenance Analytics: Analyze maintenance data to identify trends and improvement opportunities
4.3.3 Digital Inspections & Telematics
Digital Inspections: Conduct pre-trip, post-trip, and periodic inspections digitally with offline forms
Telematics Integration: Integrate with low-cost telematics devices for real-time vehicle monitoring
Data Analytics: Analyze telematics data to improve driver behavior and vehicle performance with AI insights
Defect Management: Report, track, and resolve defects identified during inspections with photo evidence
4.3.4 Lifecycle Management
Acquisition Planning: Tools for planning and executing asset acquisitions with budget tracking
Utilization Monitoring: Track asset utilization and identify underutilized assets with heat maps
Disposal Management: Manage asset retirement and disposal processes with environmental compliance
Total Cost of Ownership: Calculate and track TCO for all assets with currency conversion for cross-border operations
Replacement Planning: Plan asset replacements based on objective criteria and predictive analytics
4.4 Module 4: Safety & Risk Management
4.4.1 Journey Management
Journey Planning: Create and manage journey management plans with route optimization for security and efficiency
Route Optimization: Optimize routes based on safety, efficiency, weather, and security threats
Driver Authorization: Ensure only certified and fit drivers are authorized for journeys with biometric verification
Journey Monitoring: Track journeys in real-time with offline tracking and SOS alerts
Journey Analytics: Analyze journey data to improve future planning with predictive risk assessment
4.4.2 Incident Investigation
Incident Reporting: Document and report all incidents promptly with offline capability and automatic sync
Investigation Management: Conduct thorough investigations with trained personnel using digital toolkits
Learning Sharing: Share lessons learned from incidents across the organization with mobile learning modules
Corrective Actions: Track and verify the effectiveness of corrective actions with automated follow-ups
4.4.3 Defensive Driving
Driver Assessment: Evaluate driver skills and defensive driving capabilities with simulators and on-road assessments
Training Programs: Develop and deliver defensive driving training with localized content and offline access
Performance Monitoring: Track driver performance with telematics and AI-powered behavior analysis
Coaching Tools: Provide targeted coaching based on performance data with mobile feedback
4.4.4 Risk Assessment
Risk Identification: Identify potential risks in operations with mobile risk reporting
Risk Analysis: Assess the likelihood and impact of identified risks with AI-powered risk modeling
Risk Mitigation: Develop and implement risk mitigation strategies with automated task assignment
Risk Monitoring: Continuously monitor risks and the effectiveness of mitigation measures with real-time dashboards
4.5 Module 5: Cross-Border Trade Management (Africa-specific)
4.5.1 Border Crossing Management
Document Preparation: Prepare and manage required border crossing documents (customs, immigration, etc.) with templates for each country
Queue Management: Monitor and manage border crossing queues with real-time updates and alternative route suggestions
Fee Calculation: Calculate and manage border fees, taxes, and bribes (with transparency features) with currency conversion
Digital Corridors: Integrate with African Union digital trade corridors for faster processing
4.5.2 Customs Compliance
HS Code Classification: Classify goods according to Harmonized System codes with AI assistance
Duty Calculation: Calculate import/export duties with up-to-date tariff databases for each country
Documentation: Generate and manage customs documents (e.g., COMESA, SADC, ECOWAS) with e-signature
Compliance Tracking: Monitor customs compliance and flag potential issues with predictive analytics
4.5.3 Currency and Payment Management
Multi-Currency Accounting: Handle transactions in multiple African currencies with automatic conversion
Mobile Money Integration: Facilitate payments via mobile money (M-Pesa, MTN, etc.) and other local payment methods
Foreign Exchange Risk: Manage FX risk with hedging tools and real-time exchange rates
Payment Tracking: Track cross-border payments with blockchain verification for transparency
4.5.4 Regional Trade Agreements
Agreement Management: Manage compliance with regional trade agreements (AfCFTA, COMESA, etc.) with automated rule checking
Certificate of Origin: Generate and manage Certificates of Origin with digital verification
Quota Management: Track and manage quotas under trade agreements with alerts
Preferential Tariffs: Apply preferential tariffs automatically based on rules of origin
4.6 Module 6: Informal Economy Integration (Africa-specific)
4.6.1 Informal Transport Operator Management
Operator Registration: Register informal operators with simplified KYC processes and mobile onboarding
Trip Matching: Match informal operators with cargo/passenger needs with dynamic pricing
Quality Assurance: Monitor and rate informal operators with community feedback
Capacity Building: Provide training and resources to informal operators with mobile learning
4.6.2 Informal Settlement Logistics
Last-Mile Planning: Plan last-mile deliveries in informal settlements with mapping tools
Micro-Hubs: Manage micro-hubs in informal settlements for efficient distribution
Community Logistics: Engage community-based logistics providers with task allocation
Cash Handling: Manage cash transactions in informal settings with digital record-keeping
4.6.3 Informal Finance Integration
Microfinance Integration: Connect operators with microfinance institutions for loans and insurance
Savings Groups: Manage transport savings groups with mobile money integration
Informal Credit: Track informal credit arrangements with digital IOUs and repayment reminders
Rotating Savings: Facilitate rotating savings and credit associations (ROSCAs) for transport assets
4.6.4 Data Collection from Informal Sector
Mobile Data Collection: Collect data from informal operators via USSD, SMS, and lightweight apps
Crowdsourcing: Use crowdsourcing for traffic, road conditions, and security updates
Incentive Systems: Provide incentives for data sharing with mobile money rewards
Data Aggregation: Aggregate informal sector data for analytics and planning

Emissions Tracking: Track carbon emissions per vehicle and journey with calculation tools
Offset Opportunities: Identify carbon offset opportunities with verified projects

Solar Charging Stations: Plan and manage solar charging stations for electric vehicles

5. Non-Functional Requirements
5.1 Performance
Offline Operation: Core functionality must work without internet for up to 72 hours
Adaptive Sync: Synchronize data efficiently when connectivity is available, prioritizing critical data
Low-Bandwidth Mode: Operate effectively on 2G networks with data compression
Battery Optimization: Minimize battery consumption on mobile devices
5.2 Security
Data Encryption: End-to-end encryption for all data at rest and in transit
Offline Security: Secure data stored on devices with encryption and remote wipe capabilities
Authentication: Multi-factor authentication with biometric options and offline authentication
Compliance: Comply with African data protection regulations (e.g., Nigeria's NDPR, South Africa's POPIA)
5.3 Reliability
Uptime: 99.5% uptime for cloud services, with offline capabilities during outages
Data Recovery: Automated backup and recovery with local and cloud redundancy
Error Handling: Graceful error handling with user-friendly messages and recovery options
Resilience: System resilience to power outages and network interruptions
5.4 Usability
Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with support for screen readers and high-contrast modes
Localization: Support for major African languages (Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba, Amharic, etc.) and cultural adaptation
User Training: In-app training and support with offline access to training materials
Intuitive Design: Simple, intuitive interface designed for users with varying literacy levels
5.5 Compliance
ISO Standards: Support compliance with ISO 39001, ISO 9001, and other relevant standards
Local Regulations: Configurable to meet transport regulations in each African country
Audit Support: Tools to prepare for and conduct audits with automated report generation
Documented Processes: Documented and traceable compliance-related activities

Phase 1: Core Foundation (4 months)
Implement offline-first architecture and adaptive sync engine
Develop core data models and user management
Create foundational UI components and navigation
Implement USSD gateway and mobile money integration
Launch in one country (e.g., Nigeria) with pilot users
Phase 2: Pillar Modules (8 months)
Develop and implement the four core pillar modules
Add Africa-specific modules (cross-border, informal economy, climate resilience)
Integrate with external systems (telematics, mobile money, etc.)
Implement mobile applications for drivers and field staff
Expand to three additional countries (Kenya, South Africa, Ghana)
Phase 3: Advanced Features (6 months)
Implement AI and machine learning capabilities with federated learning
Develop advanced analytics and predictive features
Enhance automation capabilities with workflow engines
Implement blockchain for supply chain transparency
Expand to five more countries (Egypt, Ethiopia, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda)
Phase 4: Optimization and Expansion (6 months)
Optimize performance based on real-world usage
Implement additional ERP modules as needed
Enhance user experience based on feedback
Scale to remaining African countries with localization
Develop partner ecosystem for integration and extensions
8.2 Key Milestones
Milestone 1: Core platform with offline-first capabilities and USSD integration
Milestone 2: First pillar module (Fleet and Asset Management) fully implemented
Milestone 3: All core pillar modules implemented and integrated
Milestone 4: Africa-specific modules operational in pilot countries
Milestone 5: AI capabilities providing value in real-world operations
Milestone 6: System ready for pan-African deployment with full localization
